❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MedTrace

Q: Is MedTrace really free?
Yes! MedTrace is completely free to use. We believe every Indian deserves to know if they're being overcharged for healthcare.
Q: How accurate are the price estimates?
Our AI uses official CGHS 2024 rates, IRDAI approved caps, and Jan Aushadhi prices. We show ranges instead of single prices for better accuracy across different cities and hospital types.
Q: Is my medical bill data safe?
Absolutely. Your bill is processed in real-time and immediately deleted. We never store any patient data on our servers. All processing is encrypted end-to-end.
Q: Can I use this to dispute a bill?
Yes! Our AI generates a detailed fraud report with exact overcharge amounts and CGHS/IRDAI references that you can use when disputing with your hospital or insurance provider.
Q: What formats are supported?
We support JPG, PNG, and PDF files. You can also take a photo directly from your phone's camera.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority

Data Collection
We only process the medical bill image you upload. No personal information is collected or stored.
Data Processing
Your bill is analyzed using AI in real-time. The image and extracted data are immediately deleted after analysis is complete.

We do not store any user data, medical records, or bill images on our servers. All analysis results exist only in your browser session.
Third-Party Sharing
We never share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. Your medical and financial information stays with you.
Encryption
All data transmission is protected with 256-bit AES encryption and SSL/TLS protocols, the same security used by major banks.

🏥 About MedTrace

India's first agentic AI auditor for hospital bills

Our Mission
To empower every Indian with the tools to detect and fight hospital billing fraud. Indians lose over ₹10,000 Crore annually to healthcare overcharging — we're here to stop that.
How It Works
MedTrace uses a chain of 8 specialised AI agents powered by Google Gemini and Groq to analyse every line item on your bill against official CGHS rates, IRDAI caps, and real-time market prices.
Technology
Built with Gemini Vision AI for OCR, Groq-powered LLMs for chat, and a multi-agent architecture that delivers results in under 20 seconds.
